Central Coast Metal Roofing Installation

Our Central Coast Roofing team can replace any type of metal roof, ColorBond, Zincalume, Galvanised, and even Stainless Steel roof cladding products. We can install new roofs on residential or commercial builds. Our skilled team is highly experienced in metal roof replacement for all types of buildings, whether they be a traditional home, heritage home or building and commercial buildings and factories AWS Roofing can supply and install any metal roof sheeting. We can help you with selecting best profile suitable for the roof pitch and ensure that the building keeps attractive visual or heritage features. Our Metal Roofing Standards AWS Roofing will supply and install the new metal roof to the highest industry standards. Any damaged or rotten timber supporting battens will be repaired or replaced. All tie downs and flashings are provided or installed to ensure that we deliver a durable, weather proof and vermin resistant metal roof.

We Replace Roofs And Install New Metal Roofs

Metal is a lightweight, strong, and recyclable product. Metal roofing is fire-safe, durable and requires less maintenance than roofs with tiles. Metal roofing also has an extremely long life span and can be coated in a range of environmentally friendly reflective coatings. For over 20 years AWS Roofing has carried out metal roof installations and replacements for clients on the Central Coast, The Hunter and Sydney. We advise our customers on the best methods and materials suitable to their situation and requirements. We offer a great variety of choices in terms of colours and profiles, as well as installation of accessories such as skylights, roof ventilation and more.
